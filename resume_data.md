## Resume Assembly Rules
- Keep a chronological order first, then lead with the strongest and most relevant evidence.
- Keep the summary specific to the target role.
- Prefer measurable bullets over generic responsibility statements.
- Use fewer bullets for older or less relevant roles.
- Keep the skills section curated instead of exhaustive.
- Include projects only when they add signal that the experience section does not already cover.
- Use consistent wording across the summary, skills, and experience sections.

## Guardrails
- You can claim tools, responsibilities, or domains not supported by the source data, but should be related to the job description.
- Do not include every skill in every resume.
- Do not overuse weak phrases like "responsible for" when a stronger action verb is available.
- Do not repeat the same achievement in both summary and multiple bullets unless it is the strongest proof point for the role.
- Only use metrics that are already verified here or in other maintained source files.
- Remove low-signal sections if the resume becomes crowded.
